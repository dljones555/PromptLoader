When handling refunds, do the following:

1. **Verify Purchase**: Confirm the purchase details, including date, amount, and payment method.
2. **Assess Condition**: Check the condition of the product to ensure it meets the return policy criteria.
3. **Process Refund**: Initiate the refund through the appropriate payment channel.
4. **Notify Customer**: Inform the customer of the refund status and provide any necessary documentation.
